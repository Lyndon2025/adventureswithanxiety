Adventures With Anxiety

An interactive journey through the anxious mind

About The Game

Adventures With Anxiety is an interactive story game where you play as someone's anxiety. Your goal is to protect your human from ever feeling bad, embarrassed, or harmed in any way. But in doing so, you might just drive them toward a breakdown.

This game explores the mechanisms of anxiety and offers insights into how to manage it. Through playful interactions and thoughtful storytelling, you'll gain a deeper understanding of anxiety and how it affects daily life.

How To Play

Simply use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with the game. Make choices that will influence the story's progression and see how your decisions impact your human's mental state.

There are multiple endings to discover, each offering different perspectives on anxiety management.

Key Features

  • Interactive storytelling that responds to your choices
  • Educational content about anxiety and mental health
  • Beautiful illustrations and engaging gameplay
  • Multiple endings based on your decisions
  • Accessible gameplay suitable for all ages

About The Creator

Adventures With Anxiety was created by Nicky Case, an independent game developer known for creating interactive experiences that explore complex topics in accessible ways.

The game draws from psychological research and personal experiences to create an authentic portrayal of anxiety that resonates with many players.
