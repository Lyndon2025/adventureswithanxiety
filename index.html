About Adventures with Anxiety

Adventures with Anxiety is an interactive narrative experience that explores the complex world of anxiety through a unique and touching story. You'll play as Anxiety itself, trying to protect your human from the dangers of the world â€“ sometimes in ways that aren't actually helpful.

Created by award-winning game designer Nicky Case, this game offers a personal and insightful look at mental health challenges, presented in an accessible and even humorous way that resonates with both those who experience anxiety and those who want to understand it better.

Game Features

Interactive Story

Make choices throughout the narrative that affect how the story unfolds and what you learn about anxiety.

Educational Content

Learn about anxiety, cognitive behavioral techniques, and mental health in an engaging and accessible format.

Unique Perspective

Experience the story from the perspective of Anxiety itself, offering a fresh take on mental health narratives.

How to Play

Controls

Simply click or tap to make choices and progress through the story.

The game saves your progress automatically, so you can return to continue your journey anytime.

The Experience

  • Take your time to explore different dialogue options
  • There are multiple outcomes based on your choices
  • The game takes about 30-45 minutes to complete
  • No prior gaming experience needed - perfect for all players

Mental Health Resources

While Adventures with Anxiety provides insight into anxiety, it is not a substitute for professional mental health support. If you're struggling with anxiety or other mental health concerns, please consider reaching out to:

  • Your primary healthcare provider
  • National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI): 1-800-950-NAMI (6264)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • International Association for Suicide Prevention: Crisis Centers
